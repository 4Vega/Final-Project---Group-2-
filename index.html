Welcome to Cybersecurity Basics

This site is designed for beginners to understand how to stay safe online and learn about IT and cybersecurity careers. Whether you're a student, parent, or curious about the internet, we provide simple, beginner-friendly guides to get you started.

Quick Tips for Online Safety

Password Safety

Create strong, unique passwords and use a password manager.

Think Before You Click

Avoid suspicious links and unknown attachments to prevent phishing.

Keep Software Updated

Always update your devices and applications to fix security vulnerabilities.
