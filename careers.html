Career Opportunities in Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity is one of the fastest growing fields today. Many entry-level positions help people get started while gaining hands-on experience. Below is a table showing common roles, requirements, and pay ranges.

Position Requirements Location Pay Range
IT Support Technician Basic networking, Windows troubleshooting, customer service skills Chicago, IL $18–$25/hr
Security Analyst Intern Basic cybersecurity knowledge, log analysis, Linux familiarity Remote / Hybrid $15–$20/hr
Junior SOC Analyst Understanding alerts, SIEM tools, incident response basics Various U.S. Locations $50,000–$70,000/yr
Cybersecurity Technician Networking+, Security+ recommended, hands-on practice Chicago, IL $22–$30/hr
